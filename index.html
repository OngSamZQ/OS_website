Porfolio

HLV Shipment

Arranged a HLV vessel with a combined lifting capacity of 1000 tons, for transporting piles for Topside installation. Each pile was about 100m long and weighed over 200 tons. The shipment was picked up from the Korean manufacturer and delivered to Malaysia for consolidation. We used the HLV crane to load the piles onto a barge along with another batch of cargo and then transported the shipment to the offshore field for installation.

Supply Chain

Managing Supply chain effectively, with close collaboration with the procurement team and suppliers worldwide. Shipments of material to the contractor yard in Vietnam for the fabrication of the Topside was coordinated efficiently. The material was prioritized according to the production schedule to mitigate the limited storage yard capacity. In phase 1, all material was delivered timely and met the schedule of fabrication of 5 topside fabrications.

Ship's Spares

Shipment for ship's spare and delivered them to the vessels. Inspected the cargo to ensure the seaworthy packing and product delivery according to the purchase order.
